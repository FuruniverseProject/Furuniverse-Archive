Title: Anthro Species Classifications of the Furuniverse

This file outlines the major biological and cultural groupings of sapient species native to Furoria and other inhabited worlds. It details collective terms such as Peditrans/Therians, Altertherians, Primatarins, and Berdorons, along with typical lifespans, physical traits, and behavioral tendencies.

The classifications are part of fictional taxonomy used for narrative and setting consistency. They are not based on real biology or real-world demographics.
